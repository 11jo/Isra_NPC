Introduction

This mod adds Isra, a young paladin of Sune, to Baldur's Gate 1 (TuTu, BGT, or BGEE). A follower of the goddess of love, beauty, and passion, Isra is amiable, optimistic, and courteous almost to a fault. She is also still somewhat inexperienced, and not too proud or rigid to admit when she's wrong.

Content

  • A new cavalier paladin NPC for BG1
  • Voicing
  • An extensive friendship path with a protagonist
  • Banters with the original Bioware NPCs
  • Interjections and commentary
  • Opportunities for flirtation that may eventually develop into a romance
  • Player initiated dialogue

Installation

PC & non-BG:EE

The Isra NPC mod is a WeiDU mod, packaged in an extractable .RAR archive. Simply unrar "Isra_v2.2.rar" (WinRAR) into your BGEE, BGT, or Tutu directory and run "setup-Isra.exe".

To uninstall, run "setup-Isra.exe" again and select the uninstall option.

Compatibility and Installation Order

Isra should be compatible with all other WeiDU mods for BG1 TuTu or BGT, including BG1NPC. If you do encounter an error, please contact K'aeloree (kae@spellholdstudios.net) or alternatively post on the forums.

Baldur's Gate: Enhanced Edition, Baldur's Gate Trilogy, or Tutu must be installed to play Isra. Tales of the Sword Coast is not required.

Details

Isra is a twenty year old squire-paladin with the Sisters and Brothers of the Ruby Rose, the Sunite paladinic order. She has far more experience with (and sympathy for) the chaotic good churches of Sune and Lliira than any of the lawful churches, so her outlook and personality are somewhat removed from what you might expect from a paladin. Don't expect sermons or proselytism: the Firehair's faith is something better served by building close friendships than by lecturing dogma, and Isra understands that.

Most recently from Crimmor, the Caravan Capital of Amn, she can be found near Nashkel, attempting (with limited success) to resolve the legal difficulties of the artist Prism. Aid her in protecting him from Greywolf, and she will offer to help you clear the nearby mines. Troubled by the hints of conspiracy and the threat of war, Isra will be very eager to follow your quest to its ultimate conclusion.

Statistics

  • Stength 16
  • Dexterity 12
  • Constitution 16
  • Intelligence 13
  • Wisdom 14
  • Charisma 17

Notes

  • You will need to recruit her before clearing the mines. Isra is a squire-paladin with other obligations and responsibilities, and will not feel comfortable gallivanting off with strangers without good cause.
  • Isra will not join the party if the protagonist is of evil alignment. She won't be rude about it, but she is a paladin. She can tell.
  • If you don't accept her into your party when she first offers, she's gone for good. If you drop her later, you can find her in the Nashkel inn.
  • Isra's friendship track includes thirteen talks, beginning after the Nashkel mines are cleared. Two are triggered upon resting in an inn, and the latter half of the track is largely chapter dependent.
  • Your reputation has to be 10 or higher to advance the friendship track.
  • Don't let your reputation drop below 6 if you want to keep her around.
  • While disrespectful comments won't alienate her completely, Isra values courtesy very highly and won't be very happy with you if you're rude to her.
  • Isra is intended to be a romance option in BGII for a good-aligned protagonist of any race or gender with a charisma of 13 or greater. You may flirt with her in BGI, though she won't be interested in anything more serious until BGII.
  • Isra doesn't get into lethal arguments with any other joinable NPC, though her relationships with evil NPCs are strained at best, downright hostile at worst.
